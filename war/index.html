Welcome to Music By Map

Music by Map's helps you discover music events around you easier, by map. Currently we have data about concerts and festivals in the following cities:

  • Atlanta, GA
  • Berlin, Germany
  • Boston, MA
  • Chicago, IL
  • London, United Kingdom
  • Los Angeles, CA
  • Miami, CA
  • New York, NY
  • Paris, France
  • Philadelphia, PA
  • San Francisco, CA (and Bay Area)
  • Washington, D.C.
We are planning to expand the list of cities. If you would like us to add a specific city to our list, please email us at contact@musicbymap.com

Filters

On the top right hand corner you will notice the following three filters:

Today
Week
Later

By clicking on these filters you can show and hide venues that have an event today, within the next week and later at some date.

Social Connect

At the bottom of the page you will notice Facebook and Google+. Please feel free to like and +1 us and share Music By Map with your friends. The more support we get, more and better functionality we can deliver to you.

Contact

We would love to hear from you. Whether it is a list you'd like us to add to our growing list, or feature requests, please do let us know. You can simply email us at contact@musicbymap.com
